Index of Commands
================

##h264convert
Converts a video into a good quality and size h264 encoded file.
Great for mkvs from bluray.
Adjust as needed.
####Packages: 
      - ffmpeg
      - libx264 
      - libmp3lame

##midiplay
Plays a midi for your enjoyment. 
####Packages: 
      - whatever aplay comes from
      - fluidsynth

##oggplay
Plays a ogg with aplay. 
####Packages: 
      - whatever aplay comes from
      - "" oggdec ""

##flacplay
Plays a flac with aplay. 
####Packages: 
      - whatever aplay comes from
      - flac
